use a script tag.  Can use a local version on the server, but should use a Content Delivery Network CDN

local
<script src='path/jquery.min.js'></script>

jQuery Official
<script src='//code.jquery.com/jquery-VERSIONNUMBER.min.js'></script>

Google CDN
<script src='//ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/VERSIONNUMBER/jquery.min.js'></script>
